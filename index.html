Step 2: Generate and Download CSV

4
Run the Report
Click the "Run Report" button to generate your customer data report. This may take a few moments depending on how many customers you have.
5
Download as CSV
Once the report is generated, look for the "CSV" button in the export options (you should see options like "GoTo Menu", "CoTo Report", "Show", "Refresh", "Print", "Blast", "CSV", etc.) and click it to download the file.
